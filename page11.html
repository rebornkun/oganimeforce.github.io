PHYSICAL/WEAPON SKILLS

physical skills regards to skills like punch and kick that dont use cp. although not all skills that dont use cp are regarded as physical attacks. you can also have has many physical attacks you want in your card.

Weapons are kept in your skill card, a slot can hold up to  weapon skills. Wepons and physical skills are mainly used to conserve cp while still maintaining an attacking mindset.

If the attack a weapon blocks is greater than its durability, the weapon breaks.

The hp of a weapon decreaces according to the times the weapon is used per match and also used to block.

To enchant a weapon you need gems, gems can be bought with xp. Enchanting a weapon increases its rank per 1 for each time enchnted. To use a weapon, type the weapon name followed by ''equip'' and then the weapon skill. If you then want to use the same weapon, if it is still equipped, there is no need to type eqiup again. If a weapon is unequipped,. you have to use a turn to equip it , but to unequip a weapon you dont need a full turn meaning you can play again. Weapons can be bought by any race although not all races can use all weapons.
