total concetration, water breathing, first form , water surface slash (atk) - deal 4 dmg to target, if target dies by this you gain 3 cp.

 total concetration, water breathing, second form, water wheel(neg)- when your target has a def skill active, destroy that skill and deal 3 dmg to target, you play next. 4cp

 total concetration, water breathing, third form, flowing dance (atk) - this skill does two damage from 1 to 6 targets, you can choose each of the targets(this attack must affect 2 targets). 8cp,you wont be able to use atk skills next turn.

 total concetration, water breathing, forth form, striking tide (neg): when you are been attacked by 1 or 2 opponents , neglect the attack and give them 4 damage each. 5cp.

 total concetration, water breathing, fifth form, blessed rain after the dought (atk): this gives target 3 damage in your next turn.

 total concetration, water breathing, sixth form, whirlpool(atk): this skill can only be used when the battle field is underwater, deal 6 damage to 2 targets 5cp. 

 total concetration, water breathing, seventh form, drop ripple thrust curve(trap): when a opponent is about to give you 5 to 10 damage, give the opponent 4 damage which will be recorded before the opponents skill. 4cp.

 total concetration, water breathing, eight form, water fall basin (atk) : pay 1 to 5 cp and do the damage to 1 to 4 targets acoording to the cp paid. the cp paid is multiplied for every target it hits.

 total concetration, water breathing, ninth form, splashing water flow turbulent (neg): this skill cant be used in you first turn and if you didnt recieve damage last turn, deal 7 damage to opponent, this cant be neglected.5cp.

 total concetration, water breathing, tenth form, constant flux (neg): when this skill is active you you cant use any other skills except neg and atk skills. if you succesfully neglect 3 attack skill by your opponent, deal 10 damage to opponent in the turn you neglected the last attack. 6cp.

hinokami kagura dance: this skill cant be activated unless you are about to use a atk skill that deal 5 or above damage , the atk skill cant be neglected and this skill also cant be neglected. after that this skill turns to a time skill which makes all your other skills non negletable. lasts for 4 of your end turns. 4cp.
