CHARACTER CARD

A new character card can be bought for 100xp. This card can be from another race. You can switch between character cards to use per battle but cannot switch during a battle(unless you have a skill which can do that or its part of the rules).

HOW TO GET CROWNS

Crowns are also gotten by battling

Beating an opponent in your rank

Beating an opponent at least 2 ranks above you

Beating an opponent before or at 5 turns 

Note: You can run from a battle without losing crowns by using 3xp.

OTHER NOTES:

Modes can always be reactivated when deactivated, but you need to meet the requirements of using that mode first.

Health difference based moves cannot be used by clones.

Chakra fruit can only be used by 2 clones max.

Terms:

Card- This holds your skills/moves. This is what you use in-battle. When you first join, you have a card that has the following moves, punch,kick,block,dodge,kkg,and others. your initial slots are detemine by your race.

slots(a slot can be bought with 15xp, max number of slots is varies with race). Every player has a card, do not show other players your card(except from admins) so your moves will not be predictable.

Skills/Moves/Justu- These are what you use in battle. You use the moves given in your card to fight. Moves can also  be bought using xp. There are special moves(KKG) which cannot be bought and cannot be changed when you are given in your card.

Battle- This is a fight between players in the game, there are different types of battles, including one-on-one, two-on-two(tag team), friendly battle, death batlle, beast battle, tounament battle, etc. A battle is won when a player reduces his/her opponenets health to zero.

Health(HP)- This differs depending on your race(more about this in the Races & Villages section). This is an essential factor because if an opponent depletes this completely during a battle, that battle is lost.

Charge Points(CP)- This is the amount of energy one has. This is needed to perform most moves, it also differs depending on your race. If your cp drops to zero during a battle, you can skip a turn and gain 2cp, but you lose 2hp in the process as well. note that ki, chakra and others a summarized as cp in this game

Xp: This is the in-game monetary system, xp is gotten from winning battles & can also be bought using real money. 

Crowns- These are needed to level up your character rank, crowns are gotten from winning battles. Increasing your rank increases the amount of health and charge points you have at the start of a battle.

HOW TO GET XPs

Xps can be gotten from wining battles

Beating an opponent in your rank or below yours - Beating an opponent 2 ranks above you - Beating an opponent before or at 5 turns - etc, these are some tips to getting good rewards.

Xps can also be bought from an admin (bonus will be given) Note: Crowns cannot be bought

ALL BATTLE TYPES

1-on-1 Battle 2-on-2 Tag Team Battle 2-on-1 Handicap Battle Fusion Battle Tournament Battle Event Battle Beast Battle Death Battle

During Event & Beast Battles, you are allowed to use special gains called Senzu Beans & Energy capsules. During eventmbattles you can use 2 of each while during beast battles you can only use 1 of each. To use these gains they must be bought before the battle. When you use these gains in the battle, you still play.

Senzu Bean: Gain 5 health. 1xp to buy. Energy Capsule: Gain 5cp. 1xp to buy.
